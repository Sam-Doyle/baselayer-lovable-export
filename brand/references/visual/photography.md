# Base Layer — Photography & Imagery

**Source:** BASE_LAYER_BRAND_GUIDELINES.md Section 6
**Use:** Skills that generate, direct, or select images (asset-batch, ad-creative-pipeline, social-content-batch, creative-strategist).

---

## Photography Direction

**Primary style: Dramatic, high-contrast, editorial.**

- Dark backgrounds or natural environments
- Dramatic side/rim lighting
- Masculine but not aggressive
- Colorado outdoor settings (mountains, altitude, natural light)
- Real men, real skin — no airbrushed perfection

## Product Photography Rules

- Clean, dark surfaces (stone, slate, concrete)
- Single product focus — no clutter
- Dramatic shadows, minimal props
- Studio lighting with warm accent

## Lifestyle Photography Rules

- Active, outdoor contexts (skiing, hiking, morning routine)
- Natural light preferred
- Candid > posed
- Men 25-40, diverse, athletic builds

## Image Treatment

- Hero/section images get gradient overlay:
  - Desktop: `bg-gradient-to-r from-black/70 via-black/50 to-black/25`
  - Mobile: `bg-gradient-to-t from-black/80 via-black/50 to-black/30`
- Lazy loading for below-fold images
- WebP format with responsive srcSet (480w, 768w, 1200w, 1920w)

## Non-Negotiable Rules

- No stock photo aesthetic. Ever.
- No soft/rounded aesthetics in imagery.
- Colorado authentic — real environments, real altitude, real light.
- Masculine-neutral tone — confident, not aggressive.
- No airbrushed or overly retouched skin.
- No models in studio-white backgrounds.
- No emojis overlaid on any image asset.
