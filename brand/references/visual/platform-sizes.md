## Brand Guidelines — Non-Negotiable for ALL Sizes

- Monochrome palette dominant (black/white/gray).
- High contrast (near-black backgrounds, near-white highlights).
- No rounded corners on overlays or frames.
- No emojis on any asset.
- No soft/pastel color treatments.
- Sharp corners everywhere (border-radius: 0).
- Text on images: DM Sans, uppercase, tight tracking.
- Gradient overlays on photography per brand spec.
