# Social Media Specifications

## Hashtag Rules

| Platform | Limit | Best Practice | Impact |
|----------|-------|---------------|--------|
| Instagram | 5 max (Dec 2025 change) | 3-5 highly relevant | +25% engagement vs 10+ irrelevant |
| Instagram Reels | 5 max | 2-3 highly specific | Rely on audio/visual cues |
| Facebook | No strict limit | 1-3 | Minimal impact on reach |

Keyword-rich captions outperform hashtag-heavy posts: +30% reach, 2x likes.
