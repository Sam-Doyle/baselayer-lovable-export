## White Space We Own

1. **Single SKU simplicity** -- Most competitors sell systems/routines. Only Cardon launched single-SKU (and has since expanded). "One product, everything you need" is defensible.
2. **Clinical actives at accessible-premium price** -- $38 sits above mass ($7-15) and below luxury ($45+). Niacinamide + HA + Copper Peptide GHK-Cu outperforms competitors at this price.
3. **Anti-subscription trust** -- Lumin, Tiege, and Geologie all face subscription backlash. Transparent one-time purchase builds trust with skeptical men.
4. **Performance positioning** -- Not "luxury for men" (Brickell), not "budget basics" (Bulldog/Harry's), not "K-beauty" (Cardon). PERFORMANCE. Like athletic wear for your face.
5. **Altitude credibility** -- Formulated in Breckenridge at 9,600 ft. The altitude is a proving ground, not a gimmick. No competitor has an origin story this specific.
6. **Founder-led authenticity** -- Tiege was built on influencers. Founder-led creative gets 2.3x engagement and 41% lower CPA vs. generic UGC (Aspire 2025 Creator Economy Report).
