# Skincare Marketing Compliance

Quick-reference for AI content skills. Run every piece of copy through this before publishing.

## Classification
Our product (Performance Daily Face Cream) is a **cosmetic** under FDA regulations. Standard cosmetic marketing rules apply.

## Banned Words -- NEVER Use
treats, cures, heals, repairs, regenerates, eliminates, restores, reverses, rebuilds, prevents, stimulates, increases (collagen/elastin), penetrates (dermis/deep layers), anti-inflammatory, kills bacteria, detoxifies, clinically proven (unless finished product tested), dermatologist recommended (unless formally documented)

## Unsafe-to-Safe Claim Swaps

| UNSAFE (Drug Claim) | SAFE Alternative |
|---|---|
| "Eliminates wrinkles" | "Helps reduce the appearance of fine lines" |
| "Repairs skin barrier" | "Supports skin's natural moisture barrier" |
| "Treats hyperpigmentation" | "Helps visibly even skin tone" |
| "Reverses sun damage" | "Helps address visible signs of sun exposure" |
| "Stimulates collagen" | "For firmer-looking skin" |
| "Reduces inflammation" | "Calms and soothes skin" |
| "Regenerates skin cells" | "Supports skin's natural renewal process" |
| "Fights aging" / "Prevents aging" | "Helps maintain a youthful-looking complexion" |
| "Penetrates deep into skin" | "Absorbs quickly" |
| "Clinically proven to reduce wrinkles" | "Formulated with clinically studied ingredients" |
| "Cures acne" / "Kills bacteria" | "Helps promote a clearer-looking complexion" |
| "Boosts collagen" / "Restores youth" | "Supports a firmer-looking complexion" |

## Qualifier Words
**helps** (softens absolutes), **appearance of** (structural -> visual), **look of / visible** (same), **-looking** (transforms adjectives), **feels** (sensory), **promotes / supports** (contribution not causation), **designed to** (intent not outcome), **with continued use** (replaces "permanent")

Default: "Helps [verb] the appearance of [noun]" for any results claim.

## Social Media Rules
- **Disclose material connections** (payment, free product, affiliate) -- always, using "ad" / "sponsored" / "#ad"
- **NOT:** "collab," "ambassador," "#spon." Platform tools supplement but do not replace disclosure.
- **Placement:** IG: first lines of caption. TikTok: on-screen throughout. YouTube: verbal at start + overlay.
- Employee/paid reviews must be identified. Fake/suppressed reviews prohibited (FTC Oct 2024).
- **Before/after:** Consistent lighting/angles, no filters. "Results may vary" prominently displayed.

## Before Publishing Checklist
1. No drug claims? (treats, cures, heals, repairs, regenerates, eliminates)
2. All results claims qualified? ("helps," "appearance of," "-looking," or "visible")
3. Testimonials include material connection disclosure?
4. Before/after photos not misleading? ("results may vary" visible)
5. No "clinically proven" without finished-product study?
6. No specific % improvement claims without study on THIS product?
7. No "dermatologist recommended" without formal documentation?

If any check fails, rewrite before publishing.

## SPF Disclaimer

Our product does NOT contain SPF. If any content mentions sun protection, always recommend applying a separate SPF product after Base Layer face cream.
