## Niacinamide (Vitamin B3) -- 5%
- **What it is:** Water-soluble Vitamin B3 precursor to NAD+/NADP+, critical for cellular energy and skin repair.
- **Mechanism:** Strengthens barrier via ceramide/keratin production. Reduces sebum at 2-5%. Inhibits melanosome transfer (35-68% at 5%) to even tone. Inhibits NFkB inflammatory signaling.
- **Concentration:** 5% -- the most-studied clinical concentration. Effective without sensitization risk.
- **Clinical evidence:** Bissett (2005): improved fine lines, hyperpigmentation, elasticity. Hakozaki (2002): 35-68% melanosome transfer inhibition. 12-week study: 21% fine line improvement, 14% tone clarity, 15% radiance.
- **Results timeline:** 1-2 wk: hydration. 4-6 wk: oil control, pore refinement. 8-12 wk: tone evening, fine lines.
- **Safe claims:** "Clinically studied form of Vitamin B3" | "Helps visibly improve skin tone and texture" | "Helps reduce the appearance of pores" | "Supports skin's natural moisture barrier"
- **NEVER say:** "Treats acne," "reduces inflammation," "stimulates collagen," "repairs skin barrier"

## Copper Peptide GHK-Cu
- **What it is:** Naturally occurring copper-binding tripeptide in human plasma that declines with age.
- **Mechanism:** Signals increased collagen, elastin, and glycosaminoglycan production. Antioxidant via copper ion binding. Promotes tissue remodeling.
- **Concentration:** Effective at 0.01-1%. Exact concentration proprietary.
- **Clinical evidence:** Pickart et al.: promotes skin remodeling and tightening. Studies show improved density, thickness, and firmness over 12 weeks.
- **Results timeline:** 2-4 wk: smoother texture. 4-8 wk: visible firmness. 12+ wk: density and fine line improvements.
- **Safe claims:** "Copper peptide GHK-Cu for firmer-looking skin" | "Helps diminish the visible signs of aging" | "Supports a firmer, more supple-looking complexion"
- **NEVER say:** "Regenerates cells," "rebuilds collagen," "reverses aging," "repairs at cellular level"

## Panthenol (Vitamin B5)
- **What it is:** Provitamin B5 -- a humectant and emollient that converts to pantothenic acid in skin.
- **Mechanism:** Essential for coenzyme A synthesis. Attracts/holds moisture. Supports barrier integrity via lipid synthesis. Especially effective post-shave.
- **Concentration:** Effective at 1-5%. Exact concentration proprietary.
- **Clinical evidence:** Decades of use. Proven improved hydration, reduced TEWL, accelerated barrier recovery. Effective for irritated/compromised skin.
- **Results timeline:** Immediate: soothing, reduced tightness. 1-2 wk: hydration, less flaking. 4+ wk: sustained barrier support.
- **Safe claims:** "Helps soothe and condition skin" | "Supports skin's natural moisture barrier" | "Helps calm irritated skin"
- **NEVER say:** "Heals skin," "repairs damaged tissue," "treats eczema or dermatitis"

## Centella Asiatica
- **What it is:** Medicinal herb (cica/tiger grass) rich in four triterpenoid saponins: asiaticoside, madecassoside, asiatic acid, madecassic acid.
- **Mechanism:** Asiaticoside stimulates type I collagen in fibroblasts. Madecassoside inhibits inflammatory cytokines, calming redness. Provides antioxidant protection.
- **Concentration:** Effective at 0.1-1%. Exact concentration proprietary.
- **Clinical evidence:** Extensively studied in Asian dermatology. Improved wound healing markers, reduced redness, improved hydration. Effective for sensitive skin.
- **Results timeline:** 1-2 wk: reduced redness. 4 wk: improved texture. 8+ wk: sustained calming benefits.
- **Safe claims:** "Calms and soothes even sensitive skin" | "Helps reduce the visible appearance of redness" | "Supports a healthier-looking complexion"
- **NEVER say:** "Anti-inflammatory," "treats rosacea," "heals irritation," "repairs damaged skin"

## Squalane
- **What it is:** Fully saturated, plant-derived oil identical to ~13% of human sebum.
- **Mechanism:** Reinforces lipid bilayer barrier. Increases moisture up to 40%, reduces TEWL. Penetration enhancer for other actives. Non-comedogenic -- breaks dehydration-overproduction oil cycle.
- **Concentration:** 2-10% typical. Exact concentration proprietary.
- **Clinical evidence:** J. Cosmetic Dermatology: +40% moisture, significant TEWL reduction. Confirmed non-comedogenic. Antioxidant singlet oxygen quencher.
- **Results timeline:** Immediate: soft, no grease. 1-2 wk: hydration. 2-4 wk: normalized oil. 4-8 wk: barrier improvement.
- **Safe claims:** "Biomimetic moisturizer that mirrors your skin's natural oils" | "Non-comedogenic" | "Lightweight hydration that absorbs instantly" | "Plant-derived squalane for weightless moisture"
- **NEVER say:** "Repairs skin barrier," "penetrates deep into skin," "cures dryness"

## Hyaluronic Acid
- **What it is:** Naturally occurring glycosaminoglycan holding 1,000x its weight in water -- skin's primary moisture reservoir.
- **Mechanism:** Binds water from environment and deeper layers to surface. Creates moisture-retaining film reducing TEWL. Multi-weight formulations (high MW: surface, low MW: deeper) deliver best results.
- **Concentration:** Effective at 0.1-2%. Exact concentration proprietary.
- **Clinical evidence:** RCT (n=40): significant wrinkle improvement at 30 days. RCT (n=65): hydration + elasticity vs. placebo at 60 days. Pavicic (2011): multi-weight superior. Bravo (2022): systematic review confirmed all benefits.
- **Results timeline:** Immediate: plumped, hydrated. 1-2 wk: texture. 2-4 wk: fine line reduction. 8-12 wk: elasticity.
- **Safe claims:** "Holds up to 1,000x its weight in water" | "Delivers deep, lasting hydration" | "Helps plump and smooth the appearance of fine lines" | "Visibly plumper, more hydrated skin"
- **NEVER say:** "Eliminates wrinkles," "restores youthful skin," "penetrates the dermis"

## Ingredient Synergy: Protect + Correct + Hydrate

The 6 ingredients (plus SPF 30) work as an integrated system, not isolated actives:

**PROTECT** -- Daily shield against the two primary causes of visible skin aging:
- SPF 30 Broad Spectrum: UV defense (blocks 97% UVB)
- Squalane: antioxidant singlet oxygen quencher
- Centella Asiatica: antioxidant and calming protection

**CORRECT** -- Address existing visible concerns:
- Niacinamide 5%: tone, texture, pores, oil control
- Copper Peptide GHK-Cu: firmer-looking skin, visible aging signs

**HYDRATE** -- Moisture that lasts without heaviness:
- Hyaluronic Acid: deep water-binding humectant
- Squalane: barrier-sealing emollient, penetration enhancer for other actives
- Panthenol: soothing humectant, barrier support

This is why one product replaces a multi-step routine: moisturizer + serum + barrier cream + sunscreen.
