| Metric | Poor | Average | Good | Excellent |
|--------|------|---------|------|-----------|
| CPM | >$18 | $12-18 | $8-12 | <$8 |

| Hold Rate (ThruPlay / 3s views) | <15% | 20% | 25%+ | 35%+ |

### Frequency Caps by Audience

| Audience | Max Frequency | CPA Range | ROAS Range |
|----------|--------------|-----------|------------|
| Cold (prospecting) | <2.5 | $25-50+ | 1.5-3.0x |
| Warm (engagers, visitors) | <4.0 | $15-30 | 3.0-5.0x |
| Hot (retargeting, cart) | 4-6 OK | $10-25 | 3.0-7.0x |

### Key Stats
- Founder-led creative: 2.3x higher engagement, 41% lower CPA vs generic UGC
- Static image avg lifespan: 174.5 days; animated avg: 102.3 days
- Reels hook rates run 5-10pp lower than Feed (faster-swipe behavior)
- 4:5 format delivers ~1% higher CTR on image ads

### Revenue Split
- Campaign RPR avg: $0.10 | Flow RPR avg: $1.94 (18-22x)
- Flows: 5.3% of sends, 41% of email revenue
- Flows drive 3x more first-purchase revenue than campaigns
- 3-email abandoned cart series: 3x revenue vs 1-email
- Email revenue target: 30-40% of total revenue
